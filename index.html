Memory Card Game

📝 How to Play

  1. Prepare your Excel: Create an Excel file (.xlsx). Put your terms in Column A and their matches in Column B.
  2. Upload & Generate: Click "Upload Excel File", select your file, then click "Generate Game" to load your cards.
  3. Deal Cards: Click "Deal Cards" to lay the cards out. They start face-up so you can memorize them.
  4. Start Game: Click "Start Game" to flip them face-down. Find all matching pairs to win!
